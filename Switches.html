Switches

Get on/off control for virtually every type of load, including LED lights, incandescents, halogens, electronic low voltage (solid state) transformers, magnetic (iron core) low voltage transformers, fluorescents, compact fluorescents, and motors, including ceiling fans and bathroom ventilation fans.Control4 Switches provide versatile control options for a wide range of loads, offering reliable on/off functionality across various types of lighting and other electrical devices. These switches are designed to accommodate different load types, ensuring compatibility and seamless integration within your Control4 Smart Home system.

Key Features and Benefits:

Control4 Switches support a diverse range of loads, including: LED lights Incandescents Halogens Electronic low voltage (solid state) transformers Magnetic (iron core) low voltage transformers Fluorescents Compact fluorescents (CFLs) Motors, such as ceiling fans and bathroom ventilation fans Universal Control: Whether you need to control lighting fixtures, motorized devices, or other electrical loads, Control4 Switches provide universal on/off control. This versatility ensures that you can manage different types of loads throughout your home using a single, unified control system. Reliable Performance: Backed by Control4's reputation for reliability and quality, these switches offer consistent performance and durability. They are designed to handle daily use and provide long-lasting functionality for enhanced convenience and peace of mind. Integration with Control4 System: Control4 Switches seamlessly integrate with the broader Control4 ecosystem, allowing you to manage and automate your home's lighting and devices with ease. They can be programmed to work in conjunction with other smart devices, scenes, and schedules, providing a cohesive smart home experience.
