Our Solutions

"Diverse Home Automation Solutions: Enhance your living environment with a wide range of tailored automation solutions designed to elevate comfort, efficiency, and convenience in every aspect of your home."

Gate Automation

Automate your home gates for convenient access with options for both swing and slide mechanisms, ensuring ease and comfort in everyday use.

CCTV

Ensure 24/7 surveillance of your home with remote monitoring capabilities, providing peace of mind and security through advanced CCTV technology accessible from anywhere.

Theft Alarm System

Detects intrusion and prevents unauthorized entry to your home . Alerts you for any intrusion even when you are away from home.

Video Door Phone

Comfortable equipment to see, listen & talk from anywhere in your home to the visitors standing outside the door. Helpful to avoid unauthorized access to your home. Very Safe for old parents & children alone at home.

Digital Door Lock

Secure your home with advanced digital door locks that offer convenient access control through keyless entry methods like PIN codes, biometric scans, or mobile app integration, enhancing both security and convenience seamlessly.

Touch Switch

Lock and open your door using password, biometric, or mechanical key. Integrate with video door phone to open the door from anywhere inside the house

Lighting & Fan Control

Smart lighting is both beautiful and energy efficient. Your lighting automatically turns on when you need the light, but turns itself off when no one is in the room

AC Control

Adjust climate settings at the thermostat or through the user-interface from a touch screen or smartphone. Allow temperature and humidity to adjust according to the season. Even light the fireplace without ever leaving the couch.

Motorised Blinds & Curtains

Experience convenience with motorized blinds and curtains, offering effortless control, enhanced privacy, and energy efficiency at your fingertips.

Home Theatres

Design a jaw-dropping home cinema that rivals any cineplex by Incorporate immersive, high-resolution audio, high-definition video, a smart TV or projection screen, and customized lighting to create the ultimate experience.

Multizone Audio

Change the way you listen to music - forever. Fill your entire home with music or listen to a specific. playlist in just one room.

Multizone Video

A new trend in home entertainment is to distribute video throught the house. Share your entire movie library, streaming video and satellitie channel throughout the home - No need for set top boxes & multi video sources in every room

Staircase Lighting Automation

Automate staircase lighting for safety and ambiance with motion sensors, dimmers, and timers, enhancing convenience and energy efficiency seamlessly.

PIR Sensor for Restrooms

Magnetic sensors to detect LPG Gas, smoke, heat, fire and alerts at panic situation. PIR sensors detects motion and helps to switch the lights on or off..

Central Vacuum System

A central vacuum system is a built-in cleaning solution with outlets throughout the house for vacuuming convenience.

Smoke & Gas Leak Sensors

Device used to detect and warn people through visual and audio appliance during smoke or fire emergencies by raising bell or sounder alarm.

Water Level Controllers

Efficiently manage water levels with automated controllers, ensuring precise monitoring, preventing overflow, and optimizing usage for enhanced convenience and conservation in residential and commercial settings.

Massage Chair

Experience ultimate relaxation with our advanced massage sofa. Designed for comfort and therapeutic benefits, it combines soothing vibrations and customizable settings to rejuvenate both body and mind effortlessly at home.

Cleaning Robots

Cleaning robots automate household chores like vacuuming and mopping. They use sensors to navigate and clean floors efficiently. Popular models include Roomba for vacuuming and Braava for mopping, enhancing convenience and cleanliness.

Starry Ceiling Lights

Starry ceiling lights or sky lighting are fixtures that simulate a night sky with stars indoors. They use LED technology and optical fibers to create a realistic starry effect on ceilings, enhancing ambiance and creating a tranquil atmosphere.
