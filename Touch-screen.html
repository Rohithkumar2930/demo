Controller

Control4 Touchscreens offer a complete view and dedicated control of your entire home. Unlike traditional touchscreens, you won't need to find and launch an app; control of your home is instantaneous. Conveniently manage lighting, security, music, televisions, temperature, shades, and more. Their bright, high-resolution screens, fast processors, advanced microphone array, and high-grade speakers are excellent for security cameras and video doorbells, as well as intercom from room to room, or from home to mobile device. Choose from 8” or 10” in glossy black or white, and tabletop or in-wall mounting options. Control4 Touchscreens are not just functional; they are designed to elevate your smart home experience. Whether you’re managing security cameras, adjusting room temperatures, streaming music, or communicating with family members via intercom, these touchscreens provide a seamless and enjoyable user experience. Ideal for both new installations and retrofit projects, Control4 Touchscreens are backed by Control4’s reputation for reliability and innovation in smart home technology. They cater to the needs of homeowners who value convenience, aesthetic integration, and cutting-edge functionality in their living spaces.

Key features of Control4 Touchscreens include:

Instant Control: With Control4 Touchscreens, controlling your home environment is immediate and intuitive. There’s no need to navigate through multiple apps; everything is conveniently accessible from one unified interface.
High-Resolution Screens: The bright and high-resolution screens provide clear visibility and sharp details, enhancing your interaction and ensuring readability from various angles.
Fast Processors: Equipped with powerful processors, Control4 Touchscreens deliver swift responsiveness and seamless operation, ensuring that commands and adjustments are executed promptly.
Advanced Microphone Array: Incorporating an advanced microphone array, these touchscreens support sophisticated voice control capabilities. This feature enables convenient interaction with security cameras, video doorbells, and intercom systems, facilitating communication from room to room or even remotely from a mobile device.
High-Grade Speakers: Integrated high-grade speakers ensure exceptional audio quality, enhancing the functionality of features such as intercom communication and music playback throughout your home.
